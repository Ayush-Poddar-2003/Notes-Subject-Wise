### Characteristics Of Big Data(5 V’s)

Back in 2001, Gartner analyst **Doug Laney** listed the 3 ‘V’s of Big Data –  
Variety, Velocity, and Volume

1. VOLUME
2. VELOCITY
3. VARIETY
4. VARACITY
5. VALUE
